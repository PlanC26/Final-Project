AMRUT / KOZHIKODE MASTER PLAN – RELEVANT EXTRACT (VOLUME 3)

CITY DEVELOPMENT OBJECTIVE:
All developments in Kozhikode City shall conform to the Master Plan 2040 and Development Regulations issued by the Local Self Government Department.

--------------------------------
ROADS & TRANSPORT INFRASTRUCTURE
--------------------------------
Major arterial roads and widening projects are approved under the Master Plan.
Key roads include:
- Mavoor Road (Proposed width: 30m)
- Kannur Road (24m)
- Madras Road (24m)
- Wayanad Road (30m)
- Francis Road – Methottuthazham Road
- Coastal Road I & II
- Meenchantha – Beypore Road

Road widening, resurfacing, drainage correction, and safety improvements are permitted.
Construction within road widening zones is restricted.
Road safety, visibility, pedestrian access, and drainage are priority considerations.

--------------------------------
DRAINAGE, CANALS & FLOOD MITIGATION
--------------------------------
Construction is restricted near:
- Rivers: Chaliyar, Kallai, Korapuzha, Feroke River
- Cannoli Canal and natural drains

Minimum buffer distances are mandated from canals and drains.
Flood mitigation and disaster management projects are permitted in all zones.
Blocked drains, waterlogging, and flood risks fall under disaster mitigation and public safety.

--------------------------------
SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------
Solid waste treatment plants are permitted under:
- Special Zone B (Garbage Treatment Plant)

Waste management units, garbage treatment, and public utility structures are permitted uses in most zones.
Uncontrolled dumping and garbage accumulation pose public health risks.

--------------------------------
RISK AREAS & PUBLIC SAFETY
--------------------------------
High-risk and medium-risk zones require:
- Elevated plinth levels
- Disaster mitigation measures
- Structural safety compliance

Public safety issues such as road damage, flooding, traffic obstruction, and sanitation hazards are considered high priority.

--------------------------------
PUBLIC UTILITIES & ESSENTIAL SERVICES
--------------------------------
Permitted in all zones:
- Water supply infrastructure
- Sewerage systems
- Drainage correction
- Waste management units
- Public utility buildings

Emergency repair works are allowed irrespective of zoning.

--------------------------------
ALIGNMENT LOGIC FOR DECISION SYSTEM
--------------------------------
If a citizen complaint involves:
- Road damage → aligns with Road Development & Transport Infrastructure
- Drain blockage / flooding → aligns with Drainage & Disaster Mitigation
- Garbage accumulation → aligns with Solid Waste Management
- Traffic obstruction / safety hazard → aligns with Public Safety & Transport
- Water stagnation / health risk → aligns with Public Health & Infrastructure

Such complaints should be marked as:
"Aligned with AMRUT / Kozhikode Master Plan objectives"
